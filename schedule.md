## Lab 2 Hard Stop — Rescue Branch

Lab 2 runs as a **timed core sprint**. At 13:50 there is a hard stop regardless of progress. Anyone who hasn't finished the core task copies the completed file from the `rescue/` folder for their language into their project folder, commits, and pushes (see the [rescue README](rescue/README.md) for details). Participants still own the CI and deploy steps in Labs 3 and 4.

## Vibecoding — Droppable

If Lab 3 runs long (CI labs often do), Vibecoding is the natural sacrifice. 10 minutes is a fine demo. If you are 15 minutes behind at 15:35, drop it entirely — participants will not miss it if they are in the middle of CI. The live deployed URL matters more.
